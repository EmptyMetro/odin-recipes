Scallion Chicken

Description

The chicken will almost resemble Chinese Bourbon chicken. It’s sweet, it’s salty, and with the tang of the onions and fragrance of the ginger and garlic, it’s seriously addicting. I serve mine over brown rice but you can also eat it as a lettuce wrap.

Ingredients

    For the meat:

  • 4 pieces chicken thigh
  • salt and pepper
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1.5 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp garlic, minced

    • For the sauce:

  • 2 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp of oyster sauce
  • .25 tbsp sugar
  • 3 green onion, chopped
  • .5 tbsp ginger, grated

Steps

  1. Season the chicken with soy sauce, minced garlic, salt and pepper, and coat the chicken with a thin layer of cornstarch on both sides.
  2. In a pan, drizzle in oil and turn the heat to medium-high. Once the pan is hot, add the chicken, skin side facing down.
  3. Pan-fry the chicken for 7 minutes on each side or until crispy and golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  4. Using the same pan, add in soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, green onion, garlic, and ginger. Turn the heat on medium and simmer for 30 seconds, or until fragrant.
  5. Drizzle the sauce ontop of the chicken and enjoy!
